As a developer, I’m driven by curiosity and the thrill of problem-solving. I’ve always been fascinated by how things work, which led me to experiment with building websites, hosting my own servers, and developing various software projects from a young age. I’m not just about coding for the sake of it, I love to explore, break things down, and make them better. I find a great sense of satisfaction developing solutions and I'm severely set on numbers and statistics, be it users or revenue.

I’m comfortable working with PHP, Java & TypeScript, as I appreciate their logical structure. I enjoy creating solutions that are efficient, scalable, and secure, and I’m always learning new things working on backend systems using an array of languages.

My development experience dates back to 2016, when I first came into contact with Scratch & JavaScript by developing small games and trading bots. Ever since those moments I've been absolutely hooked! I specialize specifically in building integrations between platforms and thrive in back-end development with extensive experience in HubSpot development.

At the time of writing, I'm studying to become a Software Engineer at ROCvT located in Almelo (OV) currently in my 3rd year. Learning about software development & code has never been an issue for me, the hard part is designing & engineering a product to near perfection without constantly iterating over the same features.
